C on Pico Apartments is located in West Los Angeles' Sawtelle neighborhood, an intersection of everything the savvy west coast resident could ask for. Here, enjoy unmatched access to the shopping and dining in the surrounding blocks. A leisurely bike ride will lead you to the gorgeous Santa Monica beaches and thriving farmers' markets, perfect for any weekend adventure. C on Pico Apartments is also just a few moments away from the freeways and Expo, making for a quick and worry free commute.

Our homes feature European-style cabinetry, in-home washer and dryers, lustrous tile backsplashes, Cambria quartz countertops and scenic views. C on Pico Apartments' wide array of amenities means that there is something for every resident to enjoy.
